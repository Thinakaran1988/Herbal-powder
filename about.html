About Us

Dindil Organics – A product of Abhenandhan farms


Dindil, Our Organic Farm is nestled in the foothills of Vikramangalam hills, Madurai district, on the fertile river bed of Melakkal. Our farm stretches to a wide source of warm , fresh tender , nutritious herbal, leafy garden with rare plants & trees. These herbs are unique in quality which are created in nature’s way as neither Zero chemical fertilizers nor pesticides are used. This flourishing Garden products are of top quality.


Our thirst to spread health & wellness across the continents & urge to provide healthy , organic, natural food herbs at affordable prices , induced us to gear up this magical life style experience, as all our products are simple, safe, natural, & purily organic. These herbs have deep historical roots in India, though these products are not intended to diagonise, or prevent any disease.


Getting all nutrients, & vitamins that our body needs everyday, can be challenging, so usage of these herbal suppliments can be of additional advantage.


We are taking all efforts to increase our products line. Stay tuned for more of our new offerings.
